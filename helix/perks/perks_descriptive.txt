Overpower "Enhanced offensive power and burst damage"
    Domination:
    * Constant fighting will grant you an additional sharpness or power level
    * Resets if you leave combat

    Exterminate:
    * Applying 3 hits on the same target will cause your target to take damage
    * Cooldown: 30 seconds
    * Killing an opponent will reset the cooldown of Exterminate, regardless of the remaining cooldown
    * Hits will reset if you leave combat

    Combo Star:
    * Every 3rd hit you do to the same target without being hit by the target will hit your target an additional time

Unbreakable "Survivability and defense"
    Resilience:
    * Gain resistance and regeneration for a short duration upon taking damage
    * Cooldown: 20 seconds

    Endurance:
    * Tanking hits will grant you an protection
    * Resets if you leave combat

    Immunity:
    * You are immune to all debuffs, this also disables anchor

Momentum "Tempo and control"
    Sugar Rush:
    * Applying 3 hits on any target will give you a surge of speed for a short duration
    * Cooldown: 10 seconds
    * Hits will reset if you leave combat

    Dissolve:
    * Gain 50% cooldown reduction on all abilities and Refills

    Anchor:
    * Horizontal knockback is removed from both you and your opponent
